Meet Sabrina

Sabrina is the owner and sole stylist of Sabrina's Beauty Shop. The salon offers a variety of services, such as hair extensions and Brazilian Blowouts. She started her cosmetology career in 2002 by attending Arnold's Beauty School. Sabrina attended Arnold's for two years and graduated in April. Sabrina has 16 years of experience in the cosmetology field. Along with her journey, she has picked up many skills to add to her knowledge. She continues to learn every day with every new client that walks in the door. Sabrina is a fun loving person that loves to have a close relationship with her clients, and they all hold a special place in her heart.
